Seoul Garden: KBBQ

Share this with friends:

Seoul Garden

9446 Long Point Rd, Houston, TX 77055

Notes: This is our favorite place for Korean BBQ; it’s all-you-can-eat for a reasonable price, and you can cook your own meat (though the server may come help you if you’re making too much smoke). The variety of banchan side dishes are great, and everything is delicious. This place is the most fun with a group of friends and alcohol.
